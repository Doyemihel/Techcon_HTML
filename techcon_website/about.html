Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attended by thousands of innovators, engineers, and tech leaders from around the world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we aim to empower technology professionals through education, collaboration, and forward-thinking ideas. Our mission is to foster innovation and build a community dedicated to solving real-world problems through technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Alex Rivera

Alex Rivera, CTO at FutureNet, spoke at TechCon 2019 about scalable AI systems. Her innovations in distributed AI computing are transforming cloud infrastructure.

Dr. Mei Chen

Dr. Chen, a leading AI researcher at VisionTech, presented at TechCon 2021 on ethical machine learning. Her work helps shape AI governance globally.

Tomás Aguilar

Tomás Aguilar inspired audiences in 2022 with his insights into blockchain and decentralized identity. He is known for advancing secure tech solutions worldwide.
